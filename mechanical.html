Mechanical

Department

Director of Mechanical

Jared Arbolario

The Director of Mechanical is responsible for overseeing the design and assembly of all aspects of our mechanism. They are also responsible for the integration of all articulated systems, drive systems. and hardware selection, The Director of Mechanical calculates stress loads and determines stock thicknesses, hardware grades, and types. When needed, they are also responsible for submitting the Bill of Materials (BOM) to the CFO to order items necessary for the mechanism assembly.
